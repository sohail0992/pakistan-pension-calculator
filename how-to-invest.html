Al Meezan Investments

How to Set Up Monthly Auto-Invest (SIP)

Option 1: Using the Al Meezan App

Step 1: Login to your Al Meezan Funds App.
Step 2: Tap on "Invest" from the dashboard.
Step 3: Select "Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)".
Step 4: Choose your fund, enter the monthly amount, and set the start date.

Option 2: Using Meezan Bank App

1. Open Meezan Bank App > Bill Payment.
2. Select Al Meezan Investments.
3. Toggle the "Standing Instruction" or "Schedule" switch.
4. Set frequency to Monthly and confirm.

Note: For GOKP employees, mandatory contributions are automated via payroll. These steps are for additional voluntary investments.
